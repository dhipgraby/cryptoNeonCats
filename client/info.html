Info

Follow simple instructions below to design, create and acquire your favourite NeonCats


  1. Navigate to the NeonCat Factory page (will open in new window)

  2. Select one of the 2 attributes pages: Cat Colors or Cat Attributes

  3. Move the sliders of the various attributes to design your cat

  4. For a quick start, press the "Get Random Cat", and tweak it further to your taste

  5. Once you are satisfied with your Cat design, press the green button "Create New Cat".
    Make sure you have the Metamask plug-in/extension installed and that your wallet is provided with sufficient funds

  6. For funding your wallet, go here it's totally free of charge!!

  7. For help with the installation of the metamask plug in, follow this link

  8. Once you've successfully created and acquired your first NeonCat, navigate to the Catalogue page to see all the NeonCats you own
